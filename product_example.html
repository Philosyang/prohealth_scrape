    \n

    • \n

\n \n Supports Longevity.\n \n Studies have shown that boosting NAD+ synthesis in the body extends the lifespan of yeast, worms and flies. (1)\n

\n

\n \n Blood Sugar Regulation.\n \n As we age, we have more trouble processing glucose, leading to higher blood sugar. Supplemental NMN is shown to improve several markers of skeletal muscle glucose metabolism that are commonly dysregulated in people with metabolic disorders. (2)\n

\n

\n \n Supports Metabolism and Healthy Weight.\n \n Although excessive body weight can develop at any age, adults over age 40 are particularly susceptible to age-related weight gain as metabolism slows down and body composition is altered to favor fat over lean muscle. As obese individuals have reductions in both NAD+ levels and ATP (energy) production, replenishing NAD+ through NMN can help to reverse this while improving metabolic pathways to maintain a healthy weight. (3,4,5)\n

\n

\n \n Supports Healthy Cognition:\n \n Declining cognitive function with age is related to a reduced NAD+ levels that impair mitochondrial function. Replenishing NAD+ stores through NMN may be able to prevent this dysfunction. (6,7)\n

\n

\n \n Supports Healthy Skin:\n \n In our 30s and 40s, the appearance of our skin can start to show signs of age. Several studies have examined how boosting NAD+ levels in the skin prevents premature skin aging or supports a healthier external appearance. (8,9)\n

\n Frequently asked questions\n

\n
\n
\n \n What can I expect to feel when taking NMN?\n \n \n \n \n \n
\n NMN performs most of its functions on a cellular level and results are not always outwardly apparent. However, many individuals taking NMN report feeling increased energy levels, improved cognitive health, and enhanced exercise performance.\n
\n
\n
\n \n How quickly can I expect results from taking NMN?\n \n \n \n \n \n
\n While results may vary based on a variety of factors, NMN has been shown to significantly increase NAD+ levels in humans in as little as 30 days.\n
\n
\n
\n \n What dose of NMN does David Sinclair recommend?\n \n \n \n \n \n
\n David Sinclair has stated that he takes 1,000mg of NMN daily.\n
\n
\n
\n \n Will NMN help me live longer?\n \n \n \n \n \n
\n Some research shows that NMN may extend lifespan due to its ability to increase NAD+ levels in the body. Research shows that increasing NAD+ levels in aged animals extends their lifespan.\n
\n
\n
\n \n Are there any side effects from NMN?\n \n \n \n \n \n
\n Clinical research conducted on humans taking NMN shows no clinically relevant adverse effects.\n
\n
\n
\n \n What’s the difference between NMN capsules and powder?\n \n \n \n \n \n
\n Choosing between our NMN capsules or powder comes down to personal preference and convenience. Both our powders and capsules are formulated to optimize absorption and there should be no significant difference between either form.\n
\n
\n
\n \n Is taking NMN more effective than taking NAD?\n \n \n \n \n \n
\n Research suggests that taking NMN may be more effective than taking NAD due to the size of the NAD molecule not being taken up by our cells effectively. Instead, NMN is a smaller molecule that our cells can utilize to convert into NAD. Taking NAD in IV form may be more effective than NMN, but research is inconclusive.\n
